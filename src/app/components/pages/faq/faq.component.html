What is XpBids?

XpBids is a web and mobile application that allows consumers to find Service professionals to bid and complete their projects

Is XpBids liable for incomplete projects?

No, XpBids is merely a platform that provides the tools necessary for both the consumer and service professionals to agree on a projects , escrow payment and track work progress until completion

Can I post multiple Projects?

Yes, a consumer can post multiple projects and manage multiple projects simultaneously through the Consumer dashboard

How does XpBids protect payment?

Payment are kept in an escrow and would only be released to Service Professional with consumer consent or at the completion of the project

What if a Service Professional does not complete the project?

If a service professional does not complete a project and cancels the project, XpBids will refund payment in escrow back to the consumer

Does the Service Professional work for XpBids?

No, XpBids is a tool used by independent Service Professionals to bid on project

Where does the Service Professional Reviews come from?

Individual reviews come from former consumers

Can I leave a review regarding my experience with a service professional?

Yes, at the conclusion of the project

Can I cancel a project?

Yes, a consumer can cancel a project, however the Percentage of work completed will be credited to the Service Professional and the remaining balance will be refunded to the consumer

How do I contact XpBids?

You can send us an email, we will respond to emails generally within 24hrs or you can call XpBids at 855-423-4545

Can I dispute a transaction?

Yes, a consumer will be able to dispute completed projects as defined by the statement of work through the resolution center found on your dashboard

Can I change my Bid?

Yes, service Professional will be able to change their bid prior to an accepted bid by the consumer

Will I be able to speak with the Service Professional?

Yes, a consumer will be able to communicate with the Service Professional through XpBids messaging center

Could the bid price change?

Yes, a service professional might begin work and discover that the scope of the project is significantly more than the initial estimation and the bid agreement. In such situations service professionals will indicate changes to consumers and submit an addendum to the project, which the consumer may accept or decline.

What is an Escrow?

An escrow protects the fee associated with the service rendered by the service professional. Xpbids escrow is provided to you through escrow.com where your funds are held in secured, non interest bearing trust Escrow Accounts until the transaction is completed. This ensures that your transaction is protected against chargebacks, fraud or wrongly described services.

Xpbids is founded on the principles of protecting the consumer and Service professional to ensure a safe and secure transaction of services and payment.

Are my messages secure?

Our in App messaging interface allows you a level of security that protects the integrity of the bilateral exchange that you will have between consumer and service professional

Should I make payment outside of Xpbids?

Our core differentiator from other websites is payment security. Protecting your payments against risk is one of the foundational focus of our platform. We strongly discourage you from making payment arrangements outside of the secure payment arrangement of xpbids. If you do we cannot ensure the quality nor the security of payments outside of the platform

What forms of payments are acceptable?

Who purchases material?

As a consumer you can opt to purchase your own material or elect for the service professional to acquire the needed material. A consumer will be able to elect during posting of their project to indicate their preference. Service professionals will also be able to make certain that their bids are labor only or includes material

What is Statement of work or SOW?

Statement of work is a signature feature that is unique to Xpbids. SOW allows Service Professionals to clearly outline phases and tasks associated with the project. Service Professionals will complete an SOW and submit their bids to the consumers in that format.
