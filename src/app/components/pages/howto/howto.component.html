How to Post a project part 1

Posting a project on Xpbids is very simple

1. First Identify the Installation, service or repair you want
2. For example if you decided that you need a service professional to install your new television

a. Simply take a picture of your TV
b. Take a picture of the Wall you would like your TV to hang
c. Select “Lets get started” from the Xpbids homepage to complete the quick enrollment or sign into your account




Complete the quick sign up form



From the "My Post" tab select the "Create New Post" button



Simply Complete the New Post form and Submit




How can I fund my project?

We know that starting and completing your projects requires funding, that is why Xpbids have partnered with the very best sources….such as SOFI, Lending Tree and Upstart to expedite your personal loan needs. Simply select the SOFI, Lending Tree or Upstart logo anywhere on our platform, you will be routed to their website to complete the Personal Loan application. Once you complete your application and secure funding, please return to Xpbids to fund your project.

How can I contribute to Xpbids Blog?

If you are a Service Professional, we want to thank you for choosing to partner with Xpbids, we truly value the services you provide to consumers. We feel that educating and informing consumers about the wear and tear of their Home and products goes a long way in enhancing and maintaining their home or other services. That is why we are inviting you as a service professional to support the Xpbids blog community by becoming a contributor.
1. Choose a topic or subject related to your category of expertise
2. Make sure that the content is informative to the consumer
3. Make sure that the content is not offensive
4. Submit your content to submitblog@xpbids.com
5. Once we review your submission we will send you a confirmation

How do I find the right category?

All our Categories are accessible from the landing page. Simply tap or select the categories tab on the landing page to be navigated to the Categories section of the page. If you do not see the exact categories to you are looking for, you may select the all categories button to view all categories available to you.












You may also gain easy access to all subcategories from the Consumer Post a project view

What if Service Professional needs to inspect a project before bidding?

There are projects that are complex enough that requires the Service Professional to inspect before placing a bid, in such a scenario the Service Professional will inform the consumer and in addition utilize the Xpbids appointment tool to find a convenient time. Upon inspection of the project Service Professional will complete their SOW and submit a bid for the project.
